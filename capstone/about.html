About The Artist

Hi! My name is Neestha Tuladhar, and I am a recent graduate from Chico State. I have a Bachelor's of Media Arts with a minor in Social Media Production. I have been very passionate about photography and art ever since I was young, and I have worked very hard throughout the years to hone these skills so that I could create expressive works that resonate with people. With this collection of works, I wanted to showcase my photography, digital art, and graphic design skills by combining them to create multimedia collages. I hope you enjoy my pieces, and I thank you for taking the time to look at my work!
